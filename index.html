Beneath the Spikes

Documentary, 1 episodes x 8 min, Editor (Independent)

Four years ago, Hari found out that his second son Sharvin will be born premature and may not survive the ordeal. He turned to the Hindu deity Lord Murugan, and made a vow. If his son makes it home without harm, Hari will carry a Kavadi – a portable altar attached to the devotee with over 100 body piercings – in thanksgiving to Lord Murugan.

Beneath The Spikes is a documentary short about Hari, as he fulfils his religious vow by carrying a Kavadi in the Hindu festival, Panguni Uthiram.

  • Worked on shooting script, interview questions and paper edit script
  • Edited documentary with the given footage
  • Created show logo
  • Attended shoots to take shoot notes and ensure necessary soundbites and shots are covered

  • Nominee for Inaugural NYFA Best Documentary 2015
  • Runner-up at the Singapore Heritage Short Film Competition 2015
  • Screened at SJ50 Film Festival
